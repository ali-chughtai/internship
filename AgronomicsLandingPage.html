About Agronomics

Agronomics comes up with location-based suitability analysis, weather forecast, farm planning, crop simulator, soil analysis, image-based plant analysis, agripedia, Agri calculator, mandi rated, and Kissan Kollege. It also works on in-house remote sensors integration, banking compliance for crop loans, and farmer safety.

Core Competence

An online market is also available for registered fertilizers, pesticides, and seed companies where they can sell their products to the farmer. On the other hand, farmers can also sell their commodities to retailers & end users in the nearest town. It also focuses on the social initiatives that aim to promote and support Organic Farming, pollution remediating plants, "say no to food wastage," & farmer safety.
